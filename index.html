Dr. Suoqin Jin received his Ph.D. in Mathematics from Wuhan University in 2016. During 2016/08-2021/07, he did his postdoc training in the Math Department at University of California Irvine. Since 09/2021, he is an Associate Professor in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at Wuhan University.

He works on the interface between mathematics, data science and biomedicine. His main research focuses on developing mathematical theories, models and computational methods to address the challenging questions in biomedical field, particularly regarding complex diseases (e.g., cancer), skin biology and neuroscience. Recently, he is particulalry interested in developing mathematical models and machine learning methods for analyzing big single-cell genomic and imaging data.
